F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is duration of internship?

    Duration of internship will be of 1 month.

  • Which type projects we have during internship?

    You will get projects/tasks related to your internship domain.

  • How to complete assigned projects?

    You are required to independently complete one task of your choice from the task list provided along with your offer letter, within your selected internship domain. You can take help of your friends and colleagues for any doubts within task.

  • Do you provide any training?

    All assigned projects adhere to industry norms, and students can complete them independently. If you require training, you may need to seek it out in the market yourself.

  • What is the location of internship?

    All our internships are work from home and virtual.

  • What are the Perks for these Internships?

    You will get Internship Offer Letter & Internship Completion Certificate.

  • Is there any Stipend for these Internships?

    You will get Internship Offer Letter & Internship Completion Certificate. Our internships are for educational purpose and are unpaid.

  • When we will get certificate?

    You will receive your internship completion certificate within two days after completion of your internship duration and task submission.

  • How to get Letter of Recommendation (LOR)?

    After completion of respective Internship batch we will send you LOR request form along with Internship Completion Certificate. Fill the LOR request form to issue LOR.

  • When I will get access to Placement Group?

    Only students who have submitted their task and get internship certificate will receive exclusive access to our placement group after verification. We are ready to provide placement assistance.
